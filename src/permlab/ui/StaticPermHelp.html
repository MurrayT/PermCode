This window allows for the graphical exploration of permutations.

  • Type a permutation in the Permutation field, or build one by clicking in the large upper window.
  • Right click in the upper window to get appropriate menus.
  • Restrict to permutations not containing certain patterns by entering permutations in the Basis area and clicking Ok.
  • Restrict to simple permutations by selecting the check box.
  • Illustrate the monotone and singleton constraints implied by the restrictions by selecting the check box. This action is not recorded in the history.
  • Move forward and backward through your construction by using the arrows (which function like the forward and back buttons in a browser.)
