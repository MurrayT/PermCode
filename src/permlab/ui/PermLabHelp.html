Welcome to PermLab, the permutation laboratory. Feel free to press a button and dive in. The left hand button will allow you to build and explore the properties of individual permutations, while the right hand button will allow the investigation of permutation classes.

Although the code in this application is almost entirely written by me, many people have contributed to the development of PermLab over the last ten years including: Rhys Abraham, Mike Atkinson, Michael Belton, Samson deJaeger and Chris Monteith.

Michael Albert, 11/4/2012
